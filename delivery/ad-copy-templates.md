# CSTAR Media — Ad Copy Templates

**Framework:** Hook (0–3s) → Hold (3–15s) → Offer → CTA
**Rule:** 80% of results come from the creative. Spend the most time here.

---

## HOME SERVICES

### Plumber / HVAC / Electrician

**Ad #1 — Pain Hook**
> Hook: "Still waiting 3 weeks for a [plumber/HVAC tech/electrician] in [City]?"
> Hold: "Most homeowners in [City] tell us they can't find reliable service when they actually need it. We built [Business Name] to fix that."
> Offer: "Same-week appointments available. Licensed, insured, and local."
> CTA: "Click below to get a free quote in 60 seconds."

**Ad #2 — Social Proof Hook**
> Hook: "[X] homeowners in [City] chose us last month."
> Hold: "We're not the cheapest — but we show up on time, do it right the first time, and don't leave a mess."
> Offer: "Book this week and get a free [inspection / estimate / diagnostic]."
> CTA: "Tap 'Learn More' to check availability."

**Ad #3 — Price Anchor**
> Hook: "Before you pay for [service] in [City] — read this."
> Hold: "Most people overpay because they don't know what fair pricing looks like. We believe in honest quotes with no surprise fees."
> Offer: "Free estimate. No obligation. No pressure."
> CTA: "Get your free quote now."

## AD COPY RULES
1. Always speak to ONE specific person, not "everyone"
2. Use their city name — makes it feel local and relevant
3. Lead with a pain, fear, or desire — not your business name
4. One CTA per ad. Don't give them options.
5. Test 3–5 variations per ad set. Let data pick the winner.

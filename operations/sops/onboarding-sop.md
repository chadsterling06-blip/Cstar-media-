# CSTAR Media — Client Onboarding SOP

Complete this within 48 hours of a client signing.

---

## STEP 1 — Send Welcome Message (Day 0, within 1 hour of signing)

> "Hey [Name], welcome to CSTAR Media! Really excited to get started. I'll be sending over a short onboarding form shortly — takes about 10 minutes to fill out. Once I have that back, we'll book your kickoff call and get your campaign live within 7 days."

---

## STEP 2 — Send Onboarding Form (Day 0)

Ask for:
- Business name, website, phone number
- Target city/radius
- Ideal customer description (age, gender, income, pain points)
- Services they want to promote (pick top 1–2)
- Average customer value ($)
- Any past ads? Results?
- Competitors they're aware of
- Photos/videos available for creative (yes/no)
- Access to Meta Business Suite (yes/no — if no, guide them through setup)

---

## STEP 3 — Get Access (Day 1–2)

- [ ] Client adds you as Partner in Meta Business Suite
  - Their Business Manager ID: ___________
  - Send them this link: business.facebook.com/settings/partners
- [ ] Confirm ad account access
- [ ] Confirm Facebook Page access
- [ ] Confirm website access for Pixel installation (if needed)

---

## STEP 4 — Kickoff Call (Day 2–3, 30 min)

Agenda:
1. Review onboarding form answers together
2. Confirm campaign goal (leads, calls, bookings)
3. Agree on offer for the ad (free quote, free consultation, discount, etc.)
4. Set expectations: first 48–72 hrs = learning phase, first leads within 2 weeks
5. Confirm reporting cadence: Monday reports every week

---

## STEP 5 — Campaign Build (Day 3–6)

- [ ] Pixel installed and verified
- [ ] Campaign launch checklist completed → `/delivery/campaign-launch-checklist.md`
- [ ] 3–5 ad creatives built
- [ ] Ad copy written using templates → `/delivery/ad-copy-templates.md`
- [ ] Campaign reviewed internally before launch

---

## STEP 6 — Launch (Day 7)

- [ ] Ads submitted to Meta
- [ ] Client notified: "Your ads are live!"
- [ ] Baseline metrics recorded in client tracker
- [ ] First weekly report date set for following Monday

---

## STEP 7 — Day 3 Check-In

> "Hey [Name], just checking in — ads have been live for 3 days. Still in the learning phase but early signals look [good/normal]. I'll have your first full report ready Monday. Any questions so far?"

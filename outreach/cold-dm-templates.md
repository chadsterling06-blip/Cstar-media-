# CSTAR Media — Cold DM Templates

**Rule:** Lead with their PROBLEM, not your service. Never pitch in the first message.

---

## Template Formula
> [Observation about their business] + [Problem they're likely facing] + [Curiosity question]

---

## HOME SERVICES (Plumbers, Roofers, HVAC, Electricians, Landscapers)

**DM-1 — Seasonal angle**
> Hey [Name], noticed you're doing [service] in [city]. A lot of [service] businesses I've talked to lately are struggling to keep the pipeline full outside of peak season. Is that something you're dealing with right now?

**DM-2 — Competitor angle**
> Hey [Name], saw your business come up when I was looking at [service] companies in [city]. Curious — are you mostly getting leads from referrals, or do you have a consistent way to bring in new jobs every week?

**DM-3 — Simple and direct**
> Hey [Name] — quick question. If I could show you how to get 10–15 booked jobs from Facebook ads every month without touching anything yourself, would that be worth a 15-minute call?

**DM-4 — Pain point**
> Hey [Name], I work with [service] businesses and the #1 thing I hear is leads are either feast or famine — either slammed or dead slow. Sound familiar at all?

**DM-5 — Social proof angle**
> Hey [Name], I've been running Facebook ads for [service] companies and just helped one in [similar city] go from 4 leads/week to 18. Not sure if it's a fit for you but thought it was worth a message.

---

## HEALTH & WELLNESS (Dentists, Chiropractors, Gyms, Med Spas)

**DM-6 — New patient angle**
> Hey [Name], do you find most of your new patients still come from referrals, or have you had any success getting them through paid ads?

**DM-7 — Capacity angle**
> Hey [Name], noticed [practice name] — are you currently at capacity or still looking to bring in more [patients/members] each month?

**DM-8 — Specific offer**
> Hey [Name], I help [dentists/gyms/med spas] fill their schedule with qualified leads using Facebook ads. Most of my clients see results in the first 30 days. Would a free audit of your current marketing be useful?

---

## AUTOMOTIVE (Auto Repair, Detailing, Tire Shops)

**DM-9 — Slow days angle**
> Hey [Name], do you find certain days of the week are slower than others for [shop name]? A lot of shop owners I talk to say Monday–Tuesday are dead. Curious if that's your experience.

**DM-10 — Direct**
> Hey [Name], I run Facebook ads specifically for auto shops and detailers. Helped a shop in [similar city] add 22 new customers last month. Worth a quick chat?

---

## BEAUTY & PERSONAL CARE (Salons, Barbershops, Nail Salons)

**DM-11 — Booking angle**
> Hey [Name], is [salon name] fully booked right now or are you still looking to fill more slots each week?

**DM-12 — Retention angle**
> Hey [Name], most salons I talk to say getting new clients isn't the hard part — it's getting them to come back. Is that something you're focused on or more on new client growth?

---

## FOLLOW-UP TEMPLATES (use if no reply after 3–5 days)

**Follow-up 1**
> Hey [Name], just wanted to bump this up — did you get a chance to see my last message?

**Follow-up 2**
> Hey [Name], totally understand if now isn't the right time. If things ever slow down and you want to talk about getting more consistent leads, feel free to reach back out.

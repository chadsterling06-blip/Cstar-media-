# CSTAR Media — Discovery Call Script (45 min)

**Framework:** SPIN Selling — Situation → Problem → Implication → Need-Payoff
**Rule:** NEVER pitch before diagnosing. Ask questions first. Always.

---

## PRE-CALL PREP (5 min before)
- [ ] Look up their business on Google/Facebook
- [ ] Note: how many reviews, what they sell, any ads running?
- [ ] Have this script open in front of you

---

## OPENING (0–2 min)

> "Hey [Name], thanks for jumping on. I've got us blocked for about 45 minutes — does that still work for you?"

> "Before I dive into anything on my end, I want to make sure this call is actually useful for YOU. So I'm going to ask you some questions first, get a feel for where you're at, and if I think I can help I'll tell you exactly how. Sound good?"

---

## SITUATION — Understand where they are now (5–10 min)

> "So tell me a little about [business name] — how long have you been running it?"

> "How are you currently getting most of your leads or new customers?"

> "Are you running any paid ads right now — Facebook, Google, anything like that?"

> "How many new clients/jobs/customers are you bringing in on average per month?"

> "What does a typical new customer spend with you?"

---

## PROBLEM — Uncover the pain (10–15 min)

> "What's the biggest challenge you're facing right now when it comes to getting new customers consistently?"

> "Is your lead flow pretty consistent month to month, or does it go up and down?"

> "Have you tried paid ads before? How did that go?"

> "What happens to your business in the slow months — do you have anything that keeps leads coming in?"

> "If you had to pick one thing that's holding your growth back right now, what would it be?"

---

## IMPLICATION — Make the problem feel real (5–10 min)

> "So if the lead flow stays inconsistent like this for the next 6 months, what does that look like for you?"

> "How much revenue do you think you're leaving on the table every month because of that gap?"

> "And if you had to keep relying on referrals — is that something you can realistically scale?"

> "What happens if a competitor in [city] starts running ads and goes after the same customers you're after?"

---

## NEED-PAYOFF — Let them sell themselves (5 min)

> "If you had a consistent way to bring in 10–15 new qualified leads every month on autopilot — what would that mean for your business?"

> "What would that free you up to focus on?"

> "How quickly would you want to see that kind of result?"

---

## THE PITCH (only after diagnosing) (5 min)

> "Okay, based on everything you've told me — [summarize their pain in their words] — I think I can help. Here's what I do:"

> "I run Facebook and Instagram ads specifically for [their niche] businesses. I handle everything — the strategy, the copy, the creative, the targeting. You don't touch anything."

> "My goal for every client is 10+ qualified leads in the first 30 days. If I don't hit that, I don't feel right charging you."

> "It's $1,500 a month, month to month, no long contracts. You can cancel anytime."

> "Any questions before I tell you what the next step looks like?"

---

## CLOSING (5 min)

> "So the way this works is — if this sounds like a fit, we'd start with an onboarding call where I learn everything about your business, your ideal customer, what's worked before. Then I get the campaigns live within 7 days. You'll see the first leads coming in within 2 weeks."

> "Does that timeline work for you?"

> "Are you ready to move forward today, or is there something you need to think through first?"

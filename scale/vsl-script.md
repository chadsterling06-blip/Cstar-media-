# CSTAR Media — VSL Script (Video Sales Letter)
# Length: 8-12 minutes
# Framework: Problem > Agitate > Mechanism > Proof > Offer > CTA
# Use with HeyGen (AI avatar) or record yourself
# Voiceover: ElevenLabs

---

## BEFORE YOU RECORD
- [ ] Offer is proven (at least 2-3 client results)
- [ ] Close rate above 25%
- [ ] Churn under 10%/month
- [ ] You know your cost-per-call target

---

## PART 1 — HOOK (0:00–0:30)
*Grab attention in the first 5 seconds or they leave*

> "If you own a local service business and you're tired of slow months, inconsistent leads, and watching competitors show up everywhere while you're stuck waiting on referrals — stay with me for the next 8 minutes because I'm going to show you exactly how we fix that."

**Visual:** Your face or logo. Clean background. Energy is calm and confident.

---

## PART 2 — PROBLEM (0:30–2:00)
*Make them feel understood. Name their exact pain.*

> "Here's what most local business owners are dealing with right now."

> "Monday morning comes around and you're staring at a light schedule. You've got a great service. You do good work. Your customers love you. But getting a consistent flow of NEW customers? That's the part nobody teaches you."

> "You've probably tried a few things. Maybe you boosted a post on Facebook and got nothing. Maybe you hired someone to run ads and burned $1,000 with nothing to show for it. Maybe you're still just relying on Google My Business and hoping people find you."

> "And the worst part? You KNOW your competitors are getting those customers. You see them everywhere. Their ads, their posts, their reviews. And you're doing better work — but they're winning the visibility game."

> "That gap — between the quality of your service and the number of new customers walking through your door — that's what we solve."

---

## PART 3 — AGITATE (2:00–3:30)
*Make the pain of doing nothing feel real*

> "Here's what happens if nothing changes."

> "Slow months keep coming. You ride the wave of busy season and then scramble in the off months. Your best employees get nervous when work dries up. You start discounting your prices just to keep the phone ringing. And every month you're not running a real paid acquisition system, your competitors are pulling ahead."

> "I talk to business owners every week who have been putting this off for 6, 12, sometimes 24 months. And every single one of them says the same thing — 'I wish I'd done this sooner.'"

> "The market isn't slowing down. The only question is whether the leads in your city go to you or to someone else."

---

## PART 4 — MECHANISM (3:30–5:30)
*Introduce your unique approach. Why does it work?*

> "So what actually works for local service businesses in 2025?"

> "Not SEO — that takes 6-12 months. Not cold calling — nobody answers. Not just posting on social — organic reach is almost zero."

> "What works is a focused, properly built Meta ads system. Facebook and Instagram. Done right."

> "Here's why. There are over 3 billion people on Meta. In your city alone, your ideal customers — homeowners, people with money, people who need your service right now — they're scrolling every single day. The only question is whether your business shows up while they're scrolling or someone else's does."

> "At CSTAR Media, we build what we call a Precision Lead System. Here's how it works:"

> "Step one — we identify exactly who your ideal customer is. Not just age and location. We go deep on what they're worried about, what they've tried, what would make them pick up the phone today."

> "Step two — we build ads that speak directly to that person. Not generic 'call us today' ads. Ads that hit a nerve. That make them stop scrolling and think 'this is exactly what I need.'"

> "Step three — we send those people to a simple, fast landing page or lead form that captures their info and gets them to take action. No friction. No confusion."

> "Step four — we optimize every week. We look at what's working, cut what isn't, and scale what is. Your results compound over time."

> "The result? A predictable, consistent flow of qualified leads every single month — without you lifting a finger."

---

## PART 5 — PROOF (5:30–7:00)
*Show results. Use real client stories (anonymized if needed).*

> "Let me show you what this looks like in the real world."

> "[Client type #1 — e.g. HVAC company in Dallas]: When they came to us, they were getting maybe 4-5 leads a week from referrals and Google. Inconsistent. Stressful. Within 30 days of running our system, they were getting 15-20 qualified leads per week. Their schedule was booked out 3 weeks in advance for the first time ever."

> "[Client type #2 — e.g. Med spa in Chicago]: They'd tried boosting posts before. Wasted about $800 and got nothing. We rebuilt their entire approach from scratch — new creative, new targeting, new offer. First month: 34 new consultation bookings. Cost per lead: $22."

> "[Client type #3 — e.g. Landscaping company]: Started at $1,500/month with us. Within 90 days they scaled to $3,000/month because the ROI was so clear. Every dollar in, multiple dollars out."

> "These aren't outliers. This is what happens when you build the system correctly and let it run."

---

## PART 6 — OFFER (7:00–8:30)
*One offer. One price. One promise.*

> "Here's what working with CSTAR Media looks like."

> "We take on a small number of local service businesses every month — we keep it small intentionally because every client gets our full attention."

> "Here's what's included:"
> "- Complete Meta ads strategy built around YOUR business and YOUR market"
> "- All ad creative — copy, design, everything — done for you"
> "- Full campaign setup, targeting, pixel, tracking"
> "- Weekly optimization so results improve over time"
> "- Monday reports so you always know exactly what's happening"
> "- Direct access to me, not a junior account manager"

> "The investment is $1,500 per month. Month to month. No long contracts. No lock-in."

> "And here's my promise to you: if we don't hit 10 qualified leads in your first 30 days, we work for free until we do. Simple as that."

---

## PART 7 — CTA (8:30–9:30)
*One action. Make it easy. Create urgency.*

> "If this sounds like what your business needs, here's the next step."

> "Click the button below and book a free 15-minute strategy call with me. No pitch, no pressure. I'll look at your current situation, tell you exactly what I'd do differently, and if it makes sense to work together we'll talk about it."

> "Even if we don't end up working together, you'll walk away with a clear picture of what's holding your lead flow back and how to fix it."

> "I only take on [X] new clients per month so I can actually deliver results for everyone. If the spots are full when you apply, I'll let you know and get you on the waitlist."

> "Click below. Book the call. Let's get your calendar full."

## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Using HeyGen (AI Avatar — no camera needed)
1. Sign up at heygen.com
2. Create an AI avatar (use your photo or pick a stock one)
3. Paste this script into HeyGen
4. Select voice and generate
5. Download and use as your VSL video

### Using ElevenLabs (Voiceover only)
1. Sign up at elevenlabs.io
2. Create a voice clone (record 1 min of your voice)
3. Paste script sections → generate audio
4. Combine with slides in Canva or CapCut

### Hosting the VSL
- Upload to YouTube (unlisted)
- Embed on a simple landing page
- Or use Wistia (free tier) for better analytics

### Landing Page Structure
1. Headline: "Get 10+ Qualified Leads Every Month — Guaranteed"
2. VSL video (above the fold)
3. Book a call button (below the video)
4. 3 bullet proof points
5. Second call to action button
